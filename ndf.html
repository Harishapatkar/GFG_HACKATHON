Balanced diet plan for females



Monday
Breakfast Greek yogurt with fresh fruit and a handful of almonds
Mid-meals Carrot sticks with hummus
Lunch Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, and avocado
Snacks Apple slices with peanut butter
Dinner Grilled salmon with roasted vegetables

Tuesday
Breakfast Scrambled eggs with whole grain toast and a side of fruit
Mid-meals Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks
Lunch Quinoa and black bean salad with diced peppers and cucumber
Snacks Trail mix with nuts and dried fruit
Dinner Grilled chicken breast with steamed broccoli and brown rice

Wednesday
Breakfast Oatmeal with berries and nuts
Mid-meals Banana with almond butter

Snacks Greek yogurt with honey and granola
Dinner Grilled shrimp with mixed greens and roasted Brussels sprouts

Saturday
Breakfast Smoothie made with blueberries, spinach, almond milk, and protein powder
Mid-meals Apple slices with cheese
Lunch Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber
Snacks Raw almonds
Dinner Baked chicken with roasted asparagus and brown rice

Lunch Grilled chicken Caesar salad
